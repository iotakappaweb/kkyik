Hello!

Hello! I am Logan Dominguez, the president of the Iota Kappa Chapter. We are located at Boise State University as a part of Kappa Kappa Psi. I play the trombone and serve as one of the uniform managers for the Blue Thunder Marching Band.

Who Are We?

Kappa Kappa Psi is a National Service Based Fraternity that focuses on brotherhood, leadership, musicianship and service of the college and university band programs.
