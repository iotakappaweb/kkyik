Service

Our Service

Hey, I'm Anthony, the Vice President of Service. As the VPS, I am in charge of organizing our projects and events. KKPsi is a service-based fraternity. We use service and volunteer work to give back to our local musical community.

Projects

District III

District III is a local high school marching band competition. We help out by selling tickets, guiding bands, and judge running. The entire event is student-run.

Move-In

Each year, we assist first-year band members who are moving into on-campus housing. This allows new members to have a friendly face within the band before the season event starts!

High School Honor Band Clinic

Each February, local high school bands prepare for their festival performances. We put on a clinic to allow them to rehearse their music with experienced music professors.
